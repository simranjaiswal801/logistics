# Logistics Accounting Front-End Prototype

This project is a **front-end prototype** of a **logistics-focused accounting system**, customized from the HelloBooks MVP.  
It is designed specifically for **logistics and transportation businesses** to manage shipments, invoices, expenses, vehicles, and reports in a more industry-relevant way.

---

## 🔍 Project Overview
Live Demo: https://simranjaiswal801.github.io/logistics/
Most accounting software is generic and does not fully address the operational needs of logistics companies.  
This project redesigns the accounting UI to align with **real-world logistics workflows**, such as:

- Shipment-based invoicing  
- Vehicle-wise expense tracking  
- Fuel and toll management  
- Route-wise revenue and profit analysis  

The goal is to demonstrate **design thinking + front-end implementation** for an industry-specific use case.

---

## 🧩 Features Implemented

### 📊 Logistics Dashboard
- Total shipments
- Monthly revenue
- Fuel expenses
- Pending payments
- Recent shipment overview

### 🧾 Logistics Invoice Management
- Shipment ID-based invoicing
- Route (From → To)
- Distance and weight
- Vehicle number
- Cost per KM and GST
- Auto total calculation (JavaScript)

### 💸 Expense Management
- Fuel, toll, and maintenance expenses
- Vehicle-wise expense tracking

### 🚛 Vehicle Management
- Vehicle details
- Driver information
- Trips, expenses, and profit overview

### 📈 Reports Section
- Vehicle-wise profit reports
- Route-wise revenue reports
- Monthly fuel expense reports
- Pending invoices

### 🤖 AI Assistant (UI Simulation)
- Chat-style interface
- Smart suggestions for:
  - Expense categorization
  - Profit insights
  - Cost optimization

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack

- HTML5  
- CSS3 (Flexbox & Grid)  
- JavaScript (Vanilla)  

> No backend or database is used.  
> All data is static or dummy, focusing purely on UI and front-end logic.
